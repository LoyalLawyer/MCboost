📋 Application Tips

Start earlier than you think

Magic Circle applications require significant research. Allow 2-3 weeks per firm for a quality application. The commercial awareness section alone should take several hours of preparation.

Vacation schemes first

Most Magic Circle firms recruit the majority of their trainees from vacation scheme participants. A vac scheme offer often converts to a training contract offer — prioritise these applications.

Apply to 3-4 firms maximum

Quality over quantity. A strong, specific, well-researched application to three firms will outperform generic applications to all five every time.

Get your AI match first

Use our free AI firm matcher before applying. Understanding precisely why you fit a specific firm — not just that you want to work there — is the difference between a good and a great application.
