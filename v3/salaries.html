The £89,000 Jump — What Qualification Really Means

The leap from £62,000 (trainee year 2) to £150,000 (NQ) is the most dramatic salary increase you will ever experience in law. It represents the market's view of what a qualified Magic Circle lawyer is worth — and it happens overnight on your qualification date.

In your applications, avoid making compensation the reason you want to join a Magic Circle firm. Partners know the salaries are attractive — what they want to see is that you are drawn to the work itself. Demonstrate commercial curiosity, genuine interest in the firm's practice areas, and an understanding of the firm's specific strategy.
